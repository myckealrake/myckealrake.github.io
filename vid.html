Videography

Along with music projects I have also been involved with, or creating, many video projects.

RIPs TV was a project done for my advanced music production class. The class was split up into groups and asked, by the Rips Licorice to create a 'viral' video promotion for them. The winning group would have their video on youtube and featured on their website. My group, consisting of Skyler Gorsett and I, created this video and was selected as the winner. The music was a shorter version of a song of mine entitled Chaos & Serenity.

Thinking of You was a video project of mine to bring the life out of a song I had. Using a more creative, and fun, lighting technique I was able have a greater contrast between two different stages in the video.
