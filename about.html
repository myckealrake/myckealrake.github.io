Myckeal Rake has thrown himself into the world of music and audio. He graduated from Buena Vista University in Storm Lake, Iowa with a Bachelor of Arts in Music Production & Technology with a minor in Computer Science. He has been involved with music for the past twelve years, starting with middle school band, to owning his own basement studio during high school, to finally finding himself in the realm of Iowa where he finished out his undergraduate studies.

Besides academic projects Myckeal has put himself into a position to gain as much experience in the music industry as possible. He has done an internship with the engineers at Sweetwater Studios. While on campus he is involved with many of the plays and drama productions to aid with all of the audio needs. Whether it be the musical “The Little Shop of Horrors” or the comical drama “Don’t Dress for Dinner” all needed an audio engineer. Along with Drama, Myckeal is also involved with Event Technical Services on campus which deal with setting up stage, lights, and sound for anything from a twelve piece acapella group to the likes of country music stars Steel Magnolia.
