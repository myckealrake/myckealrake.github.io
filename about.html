Myckeal Rake has thrown himself into the world of music and audio. Having Graduate Buena Vista University he decide to assist the school that taught him so much. He worked for his Alma Mater In a variety of capacities; from teaching as a guest lecturer for the video and music production classes, managing the books and wrangling student workers for every live event, and midnight runs to keep the radio station running. From there he decided to move to the "Big City" of Des Moines to be a technology assistant for the Marriott Des Moines Downtown, the largest hotel in Iowa, with Encore Event technology. Wanting to move up Myckeal was able to secure a promotion to the high position of Director of Event technology for a Hyatt Regency South of the Twin Cities.

He has been involved with music for the past eleven years, starting with middle school band, to owning his own basement studio during high school that catered to local clients, to finally finding himself in the realm of Iowa where he is finishing out his undergraduate studies.

He has done an internship with the engineers at Sweetwater Studios and Stoner Theater Des Moines, part of the Des Moines Performing Arts. While on campus he is involved with many of the plays and drama productions that happen to aid with all of the audio needs. From “The Little Shop of Horrors” or the comical drama “Don’t Dress for Dinner” all need an audio engineer. Along with Drama, Myckeal is also involved with Event Technical Services on campus which deal with setting up stage, lights, and sound for anything from a twelve piece acapella group to the likes of country music stars Steel Magnolia.

Besides musical endeavors Myckeal also explored the the "Technology" portion of his Major and Minor as well. Between his senior project creating 3D audio in Unity, he has also made it appoint to learn as much in IT. Being promoted to Lead Student Tech for the campus IT Helpdesk. Beyond school Myckeal's experience and inserts have spilt into his home life. Automating a bit too much in his home as well as various home projects.
