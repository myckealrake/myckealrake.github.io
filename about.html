Myckeal Rake has thrown himself into the world of music and audio. He has been involved with music for almost 10 years; starting with his own basement studio during high school that catered to local clients, to finally finding himself in the realm of Iowa where he is finishing out his undergraduate studies gaining a degree in Music Production and Technology and now landing in the Twin Cities area.

Some of the artists & creatives he has worked with in the studio are South71, Liberty Hall Collective, BVU Chapel bands, The Omniscient Troubadours.
