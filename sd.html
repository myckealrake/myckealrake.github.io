Sound Design

Game sound design

I have done some work recently in the area of video game sound design. My first attempt, which is shown below, at working in a game engine was slightly clumsy and and lacked a bit in grace. I did eventually catch on and am now collaborating on a project known as The Watchers. It is a horror game were a father must find his two children while being followed by "The Watchers". For this game I am creating and recording all of the music and sound effects that will appear in the game. It was created by Jerry Heylmun and will be released on Steam late Quarter 1, 2016.
