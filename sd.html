Sound Design

The Watchers

During my final year I aided a fellow student with their Capstone project. That project happened to be called The Watchers. It is a horror game were a father must find his two children while being followed by "The Watchers". For this game I created and recorded all of the music and sound effects that would appear in the game. My favorite part of this project was getting the 'laughter' of the Watchers. This was accomplished by recording one of my professors tickle their daughter and then manipulating it in Pro Tools.

3D Sound

With the advancement of virtual reality, typical headphones and stereo sounds are no longer good enough. VR is about placing a person into a virtual world and getting them closer to immersion than ever before. I took this idea of immersing the player to make the audio fit as well. I created a project within Unity using the OSVR HDK2 headset as my basis for the project. I then set up a system to record as our ears would hear, in a room size similar to that of the virtual world, and placed those sounds within Unity. The result is the player hearing two mono sources, one in each ear, to give a more realistic soundscape to the environment.
