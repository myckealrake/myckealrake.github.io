Recording projects

Over the past five years I have been learning and applying new ideas and techniques to my recording and mixing practices. From humble beginnings in a basement studio in high school to my current mixes in a modern studio, everything has helped me learn and grow in the field of music production.

"C U Next Time" Was a mixing project for my advanced mixing classs. We were provided a Pro Tools file with blank stems and asked to mix the project. This was my final version, mixed and mastered.

"Chaos & Serenity" is a song that I composed using a mix of FL Studio 11 and Pro Tools 10 and other vatious instrument plug-ins.
